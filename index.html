Basic Information about railways

Railways changed the way people exported materials and their travel. They were first built in Briatin.

To power the trains, they used the steam engine which was invented by George Stephenson. Many new changes came with railways.




Shipping costs of materials dropped drastically after railways were used more often.

Railways and railroads really helped fuel the Industrial Revoultion.

People went from walking on muddy roads to riding in style on trains.
